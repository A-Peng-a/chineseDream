Yue opera

Yue opera -- the second largest type of opera in China, known as the second national opera, is also known as "the most widely spread local opera". Yue opera is good at lyrical, mainly singing, beautiful voice, vivid and touching performance, aesthetic and elegant.

Beijing Opera, once known as pingju, is one of the five major operas in China. The scene layout pays attention to xipi and erhuang in tone, accompanied by huqin and gongs and drums. It is regarded as the quintessence of Chinese culture, and Chinese opera tops the list.

Huangmei opera

In huangmei opera, the singing voice is simple and smooth. The performance is simple and meticulous, and is known for its authenticity and vivacity. A piece of heaven and celestial couple made huangmei opera popular all over the country and has a high reputation overseas.

Henan opera

Henan opera is famous for sonorous atmosphere, restrained, sound, clear, mellow flavor, lively, flesh and blood, good at expressing characters' inner feelings, and is widely welcomed by people from all walks of life due to its high artistic quality.
