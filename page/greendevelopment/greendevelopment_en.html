Ecological sustainability

Ecological civilization is a form of human civilization, which is based on the premise of respecting and maintaining nature and people With the aim of harmonious coexistence between man and nature and man and society, we should establish sustainable modes of production and consumption to guide people on the path of sustainable and harmonious development

The sum of the material and spiritual achievements made by human beings in accordance with the objective law of harmonious development of man, nature and society; It refers to the cultural and ethical form with the basic purpose of harmonious coexistence between man and nature, man and man, man and society, virtuous cycle, comprehensive development and sustained prosperity.

We should always adhere to the principle that "green waters and green mountains are better than gold and silver mountains." we should never seek short-term economic growth at the expense of the environment. We will not sacrifice the happiness of future generations for the so-called "abundance" of the present generation. We will persist in misplaced competition and differentiated development, and strive to build "shanshui cities, ecological industries and beautiful villages". To achieve harmony between man and nature at a higher level.

Respect natural rights and interests to achieve ecological justice, and protect people's rights and interests to achieve social justice; High efficiency means to seek the ecological efficiency with balance and productivity in the natural ecosystem, the economic efficiency with low input, no pollution and high yield in the economic production system, and the social efficiency with perfect and stable operation of the human social system and system. Harmony means seeking equal harmony between man and nature, between man and man, between man and society

The first is sustainable development, the second is a critique of the nature of capitalism, and the third is a new interpretation of the nature of socialism. From another perspective, this greatly confirms the great significance of the CPC's scientific outlook on development. The scientific concept of development pursues the overall reform of sustainable development in politics, economy, society and culture, including ideology and system. The socialist profound connotation that the scientific outlook on development can tap is far from exhausted, and the socialist ideology and culture that the scientific outlook on development can supplement are far from complete.

The biggest enlightenment of ecological socialism mainly lies in ideology and culture, that is, the inherent nature of socialism requires it to lead the great transformation of the world from industrial civilization to new civilization. We are on the eve of a new civilization. This is ecological civilization.

Three north project Three north greening project action

"Three north" shelterbelt project

The "three north" shelterbelt project refers to the large-scale artificial forestry ecological project built in the three north areas of China (northwest, north and northeast). In order to improve the ecological environment, the Chinese government decided to list this project as an important national economic construction project in 1979. The planning period of the project is 70 years and it will be carried out in seven phases. The fifth phase of the project has been started.
