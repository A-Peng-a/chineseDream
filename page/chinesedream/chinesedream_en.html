Chinese dream: the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation

Since the 18th national congress of the communist party of China (CPC) was held, the Chinese dream, an important guiding ideology and important governing philosophy proposed by general secretary xi jinping, was formally put forward on November 29, 2012.

Xi jinping, general secretary of the communist party of China central committee, first explained the concept of the "Chinese dream" when visiting the exhibition "road to rejuvenation" at the national museum of China on November 29, 2012. He said:

Everyone is talking about the Chinese dream. To realize the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is. The goal of building a moderately prosperous society in all respects by the 100 years after the founding of the communist party of China.

On October 18, 2017, comrade xi jinping pointed out in his report to the 19th national congress that realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is the greatest dream of the Chinese nation since modern times.
